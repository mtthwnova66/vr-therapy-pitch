About Our Vision

Our mission is to revolutionize mental health treatment with AI-powered VR exposure therapy. By harnessing cutting-edge technology, we overcome traditional limitations and offer an immersive, adaptive, and clinically effective solution.

The Problem – Therapy Needs to Evolve

Traditional exposure therapy often falls short due to static, pre-recorded scenarios that fail to engage patients. This results in low immersion and high dropout rates.

  • Low immersion and engagement.
  • Inability to adjust therapy in real time.
  • Patient dropout rates of up to 50%.

Our Solution – AI-Driven, Real-Time VR Exposure Therapy

We transform therapy by creating dynamic, immersive VR environments that respond in real time to therapist input and patient biofeedback.

  • Dynamic, AI-generated environments.
  • Real-time biofeedback integration.
  • Comprehensive clinical control via a dedicated dashboard.

How It Works

  • Therapist launches the software on a compatible VR headset.
  • Patient dons the VR headset and sensor-enabled smartwatch.
  • The AI listens and generates an interactive VR environment.
  • Real-time biofeedback allows for dynamic session adjustments.
  • The system adapts, personalizing future sessions.

Technology

Leveraging instantaneous speech-to-video AI, our platform converts spoken words into immersive 360° VR landscapes. Integrated with real-time biofeedback and cloud-based analytics, it seamlessly supports devices such as Meta Quest, HTC Vive, Pico 4, and Valve Index.

Market Opportunity

With a global VR therapy market valued at $11.3B and an expected CAGR of 34%, our solution taps into a massive opportunity. Combined with the emerging speech-to-video AI industry and over 4.4M therapists worldwide, the potential is enormous—especially among early adopters in Europe and North America.
