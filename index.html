Founders: Răzvan Crețu & Matei Seleușan

Demo

The Problem – Therapy Needs to Evolve

1 in 3 people worldwide suffer from anxiety-related conditions.

Traditional exposure therapy relies on imagination or pre-recorded VR, making it ineffective.

The Need for Disruption

90% of therapists say treatment effectiveness would improve if exposure therapy adapted dynamically to patient responses.

Existing VR therapy is static – It plays pre-recorded scenarios that cannot change based on patient reactions.

The Solution

AI-driven real-time VR exposure therapy dynamically adjusts based on therapist input and patient biofeedback.

How It Works

Therapist launches software on a compatible VR headset.

Patient wears the VR headset and smartwatch (or future proprietary sensor).

Therapist speaks, AI listens: The AI generates the exposure environment.

Biofeedback tracking: The software monitors the patient’s physiological responses in real-time.

Technology Stack

Instantaneous Speech-to-Video AI – Converts therapist's words into dynamic 360-degree VR landscapes using existing APIs & proprietary technology.

Real-Time Biofeedback Processing – Integrates heart rate, HRV, skin conductance (future).

Cloud-Based Analytics – Tracks patient progress across multiple sessions.

Business Model

B2B SaaS Subscription-Based Pricing – Clinics pay a monthly or per-patient fee.

Hardware Bundling – We ship pre-configured VR headsets & smartwatches.
