About Synaeon

Synaeon is at the forefront of mental health innovation, leveraging AI and VR to create immersive, adaptive exposure therapy experiences. Our mission is to make mental health treatment more accessible, effective, and engaging.

The Problem

Traditional exposure therapy relies heavily on imagination or static scenarios, which can limit engagement and effectiveness. Many patients struggle with immersion, leading to high dropout rates and suboptimal outcomes.

Our Solution

Synaeon’s AI-powered VR therapy creates dynamic, real-time environments that adapt to therapist input and patient biofeedback. This ensures a personalized, immersive experience that enhances engagement and therapeutic outcomes.

How It Works

Therapists use our platform to generate VR scenarios based on their guidance, while real-time biofeedback from the patient allows for dynamic adjustments. The system learns and adapts, making each session more effective.

Technology

Our platform leverages speech-to-video AI to transform spoken words into immersive 360° VR environments. Integrated biofeedback and cloud analytics support leading VR devices like Meta Quest and HTC Vive.
