Our Vision

Re‑imagining evidence‑based therapy for the 2020s

We craft adaptive, immersive VR spaces that respond to the physiological signals of each patient in real‑time, under the expert guidance of their therapist.

🔮

Generative AI Engine

💓

Adaptive Biofeedback

Synaeon’s sensor fusion tracks heart‑rate variability, eye‑gaze and galvanic skin response – adapting scene intensity accordingly.

The Challenge

Why traditional exposure therapy falls short

Static media, minimal personalization and limited engagement lead to 50 % dropout rates across anxiety treatments.

🖼️

Low immersion

Images and pre‑recorded videos struggle to evoke the emotional salience required for neural reconsolidation.

⚖️

Limited adaptation

One‑size‑fits‑all scenarios cannot calibrate exposure intensity to a patient’s moment‑to‑moment needs.

⏳

High dropout

Without rapid wins and clear progress feedback, motivation wanes – and therapy stalls.

Our Solution

Real‑time, AI‑driven VR treatment

Dynamic worlds, bio‑adaptive difficulty and machine‑driven personalization – all orchestrated by the therapist.

🌌

Infinite Scenes

From a crowded metro to a serenely lit rooftop, environments morph within seconds to suit therapeutic goals.

📈

Bio‑adaptive Intensity

Physiological signals continuously steer exposure levels, avoiding over‑ or under‑stimulation.

🤖

Self‑optimizing Protocols

Session data trains personalized models that recommend the optimal next challenge and timeline.
