The Problem – Therapy Needs to Evolve

Traditional exposure therapy often falls short because it relies on a patient’s imagination or static, pre-recorded VR scenarios. This leads to high dropout rates, sometimes reaching 50%.

  • Low immersion and engagement
  • Inability to adjust therapy in real time
  • Heavy reliance on imagination or static media

Our Solution – AI-Driven, Real-Time VR Exposure Therapy

We create dynamic, immersive VR environments that respond in real time to therapist input and patient biofeedback—ensuring every session is personalized for maximum impact.

  • AI-generated environments that adapt dynamically
  • Real-time biofeedback integration
  • Therapist dashboard with full control

Technology

Our platform transforms spoken words into immersive 360° VR landscapes using speech-to-3D AI, integrated with real-time physiological data and a cloud-based analytics dashboard. It supports Meta Quest, HTC Vive, and other major headsets.
