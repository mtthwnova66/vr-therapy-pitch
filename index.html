About Our Vision

We are revolutionizing mental health with AI-powered VR exposure therapy that adapts in real-time.

1 in 3 people worldwide suffer from anxiety-related conditions.

Traditional exposure therapy relies on imagination or pre-recorded VR, making it ineffective.

Technology

Instantaneous Speech-to-Video AI – Converts therapist's words into dynamic 360° VR landscapes using advanced APIs & proprietary technology.

Real-Time Biofeedback Processing – Integrates heart rate, HRV, and skin conductance (coming soon).

Cloud-Based Analytics – Tracks patient progress seamlessly across sessions.

Business Model

B2B SaaS Subscription-Based Pricing – Clinics pay a monthly or per-patient fee.

Hardware Bundling – We offer pre-configured VR headsets & smartwatches.

Our scaling strategy focuses on expanding into broader telehealth markets.
